Markovian

Mission

Markovian is an advertising technology company dedicated to producing superior return on ad spend for its clients by adhering to mathematical and statistical methods.
Its mission is to make advertising transparent for its clients.

By showing display ads only to valuable exisiting and potential customers advertisers can maximise their Return on Ad Spend (RoAS).

Our low and fully transparent fee structure further improves the Return On Investment (ROI) from display advertising.

Methodology

Our approach is based on a robust Machine Learning technique aimed at handling the sparse data situations natural to display advertising.

Markovian engages in Real-Time Bidding on behalf of its clients for desktop and mobile impressions on the AppNexus ad-exchange.

We work with information available on ad-exchanges and first-party data provided to us by advertisers. Evaluating user data enables us to estimate Conversion Rate and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) for potential customers and translate it into optimal bids on the ad-exchange.

Unlike DSPs and other market access providers we do not build and maintain large propietary infrastructure. This allows us to focus on the performance of our bidding technique and to offer our service at a competitive rate.
